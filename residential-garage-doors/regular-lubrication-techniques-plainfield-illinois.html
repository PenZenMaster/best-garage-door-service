What are the best lubrication techniques for maintaining garage doors in Plainfield, Illinois?
The best lubrication techniques include cleaning the tracks and rollers first, applying a silicone-based lubricant to all moving parts like hinges, rollers, and springs, and ensuring the lubricant is evenly distributed by opening and closing the door several times.
How often should I lubricate my garage door to ensure smooth operation and avoid costly repairs?
To maintain smooth operation and prevent costly repairs, it is recommended to lubricate your garage door every six months. However, in climates with extreme weather conditions like Plainfield, Illinois, you may need to perform this maintenance every three months.
