What is the average labor cost for garage door installation in Coal City, Illinois?
The average labor cost for garage door installation in Coal City, Illinois, typically ranges from $150 to $350, depending on the complexity of the installation and the contractors rates.
Are there any additional labor charges for removing an old garage door in Coal City, Illinois?
Yes, there may be additional labor charges for removing an old garage door, which can range from $50 to $100, depending on the contractor and the condition of the existing door.
Do labor costs for garage door installation in Coal City, Illinois, vary based on the type of garage door?
