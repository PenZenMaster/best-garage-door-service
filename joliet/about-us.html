About Us

Overhead Door Company of Joliet

The Genuine. The Original. Locally Owned Since 1978.Overhead Door Company of Joliet™ (Middleton Overhead Doors) is a family-owned and operated business dedicated to providing the Greater Joliet area with premium garage door solutions. Founded by Mark Middleton in 1978, our company began in a humble two-car garage and has grown into the region’s premier distributor of the iconic Overhead Door™ brand.Today, led by the Middleton family, we combine the personalized service of a local business with the power of a national brand that invented the first upward-acting garage door over 100 years ago. We specialize in the sales, installation, and expert repair of residential garage doors, commercial overhead doors, and high-performance openers. With a team of long-tenured experts—many of whom have been with us for over 20 years—we take pride in delivering reliable, fair-priced service that enhances the safety, curb appeal, and value of your property.
