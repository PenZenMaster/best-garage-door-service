What type of lubricant is best for garage door hinges and rollers?
For lubricating garage door hinges and rollers, its best to use a silicone-based spray or a lithium-based grease. These types of lubricants are effective in reducing friction and preventing rust without attracting dust and debris.
How often should I lubricate the hinges and rollers of my garage door?
It is recommended to lubricate the hinges and rollers of your garage door every six months. Regular lubrication helps ensure smooth operation, reduces noise, and extends the lifespan of the components.
Are there any specific signs that indicate my garage door hinges and rollers need lubrication?
Yes, common signs include squeaking or grinding noises during operation, visible rust on the components, or the door moving unevenly or with resistance. If you notice any of these signs, its a good idea to lubricate the hinges and rollers promptly.
