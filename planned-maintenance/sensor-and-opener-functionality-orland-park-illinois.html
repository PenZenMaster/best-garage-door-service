What are the common signs that my garage door sensors need maintenance in Orland Park, Illinois?
Common signs include the garage door not closing properly, the door reversing unexpectedly, blinking sensor lights, or the door operating erratically. Regular checks can prevent these issues.
How often should the opener and sensors be inspected for planned maintenance?
It is recommended to inspect your garage door opener and sensors at least once a year to ensure they are functioning correctly and to prevent potential issues.
What specific maintenance tasks should be performed on garage door openers and sensors?
Maintenance tasks include cleaning the sensors to ensure they are free from debris, checking the alignment of the sensors, testing the auto-reverse feature of the opener, and lubricating moving parts as needed.
