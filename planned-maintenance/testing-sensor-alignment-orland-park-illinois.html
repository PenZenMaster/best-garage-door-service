What are the signs that my garage door sensors might be misaligned in Orland Park, Illinois?
Signs of misaligned sensors include the garage door not closing properly, the door reversing after attempting to close, and the sensor lights blinking or not being steady. Its important to address these issues during planned maintenance to ensure safety and functionality.
How can I ensure my garage door sensors are properly aligned during planned maintenance?
During planned maintenance, check that the sensors are facing each other and mounted at the same height on either side of the door. Clean any dirt or debris from the sensors and verify the indicator lights are steady. If alignment issues persist, adjusting the sensor brackets or consulting a professional technician may be necessary.
Is professional inspection necessary for checking sensor alignment in Orland Park, Illinois?
While basic alignment checks can be done by homeowners, professional inspection is recommended during planned maintenance to ensure precise alignment and to address any underlying issues that might not be visible. A technician can also perform a thorough safety test and make necessary adjustments for optimal performance.
