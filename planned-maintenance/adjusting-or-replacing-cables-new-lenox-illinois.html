Why is it important to adjust or replace garage door cables during planned maintenance?
Properly adjusted and functioning cables ensure the safe and smooth operation of your garage door. Worn or damaged cables can lead to door misalignment or failure, posing safety risks and potentially leading to costly repairs.
How can I tell if my garage door cables need adjustment or replacement?
Signs that cables need attention include visible fraying, rust, or wear, uneven door movement, or unusual noises during operation. Regular inspections during maintenance can help identify these issues early.
Can I adjust or replace garage door cables myself, or should I hire a professional?
While minor adjustments might be feasible for experienced DIYers, replacing cables is complex and potentially dangerous. Its recommended to hire a professional to ensure safety and proper installation.
How often should garage door cables be inspected or replaced?
Cables should be inspected at least annually during planned maintenance. Replacement depends on wear and tear but typically every 5 to 7 years, or sooner if damage is evident.
What should I expect to pay for cable adjustment or replacement in New Lenox, Illinois?
Costs can vary based on the extent of work needed and local rates, but typically, you might expect to pay between $150 to $300 for professional cable replacement or adjustment services.
